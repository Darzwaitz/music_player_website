About Grace Under Pressure:

This economical trio play & feature music by Canadian rock titans ‘Rush’, including hits from Moving Pictures, Signals, Permanent Waves, Roll the Bones, Vapor Trails and Snakes and Arrows.
Grace Under Pressure are:
Peter Guy on bass and lead vocals, Adam Rawski on guitar and vocals and Mark Johnson on drums and vocals. As such Grace Under Pressure proudly authenticates the classics, employing only 3 musicians, and our secret weapon!
